CENTER FOR TEACHING AND LEARNING (CTL)
================================================================================

The CTL is FAU's central hub for student academic support on the Boca Raton campus.
It houses several key support centers under one roof.

Location: General Classroom South (GS), 2nd floor, Boca Raton campus
Website: fau.edu/ctl

Centers housed within the CTL:
  - Center for Learning and Student Success (CLASS)
  - Math Learning Center (MLC)
  - Science Learning Center (SLC)
  - University Center for Excellence in Writing (UCEW)
  - Speaking Center
  - Learning Assistant (LA) Program
  - eSuccess (online learner support)

================================================================================
MATH LEARNING CENTER (MLC)
================================================================================

Website: fau.edu/mlc
Location: General Classroom South (GS), Suite 211, Boca Raton campus
Size: 20,000 sq ft technology-enhanced tutoring center

Services:
  - FREE drop-in in-person tutoring (no appointment needed)
  - FREE online tutoring via Zoom (drop-in, weekly schedule)
  - Appointment-based tutoring for select courses
  - Specialized Calculus lab

Tutors: CRLA-certified peer tutors trained in effective tutoring techniques.
Covers most undergraduate math courses.

Online tutoring: Available for select math courses. Access via tutoring.fau.edu
(TracCloud platform). Join Zoom session listed in the schedule.

Note: Tutoring does not occur on official university holidays.
Scheduling: tutoring.fau.edu

To cancel an appointment:
  - Log in at tutoring.fau.edu with your FAU ID and password
  - Find your upcoming appointment on the dashboard
  - Click "X" to cancel (cannot cancel within 2 hours of appointment start time)
  - For last-minute cancellations email class@fau.edu

CENTER FOR LEARNING AND STUDENT SUCCESS (CLASS)
================================================================================

Website: fau.edu/class
Location: Center for Teaching and Learning, Boca Raton campus
Also serves Broward campuses.

Services:
  - FREE tutoring (drop-in and appointment-based)
  - Supplemental Instruction (SI) for FAU's most difficult courses
  - eTutoring (online tutoring)
  - eSuccess programs for online learners
  - General academic assistance and referrals

Supplemental Instruction (SI):
  SI Leaders are students who previously earned an "A" in the course and re-attend
  to offer structured study sessions outside of class. SI is offered for FAU's
  hardest courses and is free to all enrolled students.

Small group tutoring available for key business and engineering core courses.

Scheduling: tutoring.fau.edu or walk in during hours and sit at a table with your
course name displayed for one-on-one or small group tutoring.

HOW TO SCHEDULE TUTORING
================================================================================

1. Go to tutoring.fau.edu
2. Log in with your FAU ID and password
3. Find the widget "Schedule a Tutoring/Consultant Appointment"
4. Choose your course from the drop-down menu and click Search
5. Select an available session (in-person sessions are color coded yellow for SLC)
6. Confirm your appointment — you will receive an email confirmation

For online sessions: click the location link in your confirmation to join the Zoom.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Is tutoring really free?
A: Yes. All tutoring through MLC, CLASS, SLC, and UCEW is completely free for
   FAU students.

Q: Do I need an appointment?
A: MLC and Get Wise are drop-in. CLASS tutoring can be drop-in or appointment.
   SLC and UCEW are appointment-based. Book at tutoring.fau.edu.

Q: What if my course isn't listed?
A: Contact CLASS directly at the CTL or submit a request through tutoring.fau.edu.
   CLASS also provides general academic assistance and referrals.

Q: Is online tutoring available?
A: Yes. MLC offers online drop-in tutoring via Zoom. UCEW and the Speaking Center
   also offer virtual appointments.

Q: What is eTutoring?
A: eTutoring is online tutoring offered through CLASS for students who cannot
   attend in person, including online-enrolled students.
