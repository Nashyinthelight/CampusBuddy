FAU POLICE DEPARTMENT (FAUPD)
================================================================================

The Florida Atlantic University Police Department is a full-service law enforcement
agency providing 24-hour protection and services to all FAU campuses and more than
30,000 students, faculty, and staff.

EMERGENCY: Call 9-1-1 (all campuses, any emergency)

Non-Emergency: 561-297-3500 (Boca Raton)
Victim Services: 561-297-4841
Victim Services 24-hour line: 561-297-0500 (ask to speak to an advocate)
Victim Services Email: victimservices@fau.edu

The Boca Raton campus is FAUPD's largest area of operations — over 800 acres and
more than 50 buildings. FAUPD also serves MacArthur (Jupiter), Harbor Branch,
Davie, and SeaTech campuses.

================================================================================

BLUE LIGHT EMERGENCY PHONES:
  Blue light phones are located throughout FAU campuses on yellow poles. Each pole
  has a red emergency button that directly connects you to FAUPD the moment it is
  pressed. FAU police respond in person to every button press.
  Blue Light Phone Map: fau.edu/police (see Blue Light Phone Map link)

================================================================================
NIGHTOWLS ESCORT SERVICE
================================================================================

The NightOwls Program is a FREE student-run evening escort service on the Boca
Raton campus.

Call for a ride: 561-297-6695

Students can request a free ride to or from:
  - Classes
  - The library
  - Residence halls
  - Parking lots
  - Any other on-campus location

When NightOwls is not available, Community Service Officers (CSOs) — FAU students
in good academic standing who work for FAUPD as civilian employees — can escort
students to any on-campus destination.

================================================================================
COMMUNITY SERVICE OFFICERS (CSOs)
================================================================================

CSOs are FAU students employed by the police department as civilian staff.
They assist with safety escorts when NightOwls is unavailable and support general
campus safety operations.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: What number do I call in an emergency on campus?
A: Call 9-1-1 for all emergencies regardless of campus location.

Q: What if I need non-emergency police assistance?
A: Call 561-297-3500 for FAUPD non-emergency on the Boca Raton campus.

Q: How do I get a safe escort at night?
A: Call NightOwls at 561-297-6695 for a free ride anywhere on the Boca Raton
   campus. If NightOwls is unavailable, CSOs can escort you on foot.

Q: What are the blue light poles around campus?
A: They are emergency phones. Push the red button to be instantly connected to
   FAUPD. Officers respond in person to every activation.

Q: How will I be notified of a campus emergency?
A: FAU sends alerts via text, phone call, and email. Make sure your contact info
   is up to date in MyFAU. Monitor fau.edu/emergency for current advisories.

Q: Where can I report a concern or suspicious activity?
A: Visit fau.edu/report or call FAUPD at 561-297-3500.

Q: Where can I find the FAU Safety App?
A: Visit fau.edu/police and look for the FAU Safety App link.

Q: My car battery died on campus — can FAUPD help?
A: Yes. Call FAUPD at 561-297-3500 and they can assist with jump starts on campus.
