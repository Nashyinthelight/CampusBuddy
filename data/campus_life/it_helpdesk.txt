================================================================================
KEY STUDENT IT RESOURCES
================================================================================

FAUNET ID:
  Your FAUNet ID is your credential for accessing all FAU systems including:
  MyFAU, Canvas, email, library databases, and campus Wi-Fi.
  Set up or reset at: fau.edu/oit/services/accounts/

FAU EMAIL:
  All students receive an official FAU email account (@fau.edu).
  Access via: outlook.office.com or mail.fau.edu
  Used for all official university communications.
  Email system info: fau.edu/oit/communication/e-mail-system/

CANVAS (Learning Management System):
  Canvas is FAU's official LMS for all courses — syllabi, assignments, grades,
  announcements, and course materials.
  Access: fau.edu/canvas
  24/7 support via 561-297-3999 (Canvas option) or 855-691-7827

MYFAU PORTAL:
  Your central hub for registration, financial aid, grades, and account info.
  Access: myfau.fau.edu
  Mobile app: fau.edu/mobile

WI-FI ON CAMPUS:
  Network: FAU_WiFi (primary campus wireless network)
  Coverage across all FAU campuses.
  Wi-Fi info: fau.edu/oit/services/wireless/

VPN (VIRTUAL PRIVATE NETWORK):
  Required to access some FAU systems remotely (library databases, Banner, etc.)
  Setup instructions: fau.edu/oit/security/vpn/

VIRTUAL APPS:
  Access FAU software remotely through Virtual Apps without needing campus access.
  fau.edu/oit/instructional/virtual-apps/

MICROSOFT 365:
  FAU students have access to Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, etc.)
  Available through your FAU account.

GOOGLE WORKSPACE:
  FAU provides Google Workspace access; note that storage limits apply.
  Storage guidance: fau.edu/oit/google/

================================================================================
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: How do I contact the IT Help Desk?
A: Call 561-297-3999, submit a ticket at helpdesk.fau.edu, or walk in to
   Building CM Room 138 on the Boca Raton campus during posted hours.

Q: What are the IT Help Desk hours?
A: Boca Raton: Mon-Thu 7:30am-9pm, Fri 7:30am-5pm, Sat-Sun 9am-6pm.

Q: I'm locked out of Canvas — who do I call?
A: Call 561-297-3999 and select the Canvas support option, or call
   855-691-7827. Canvas support is available 24/7.

Q: How do I access FAU software from home?
A: Use Virtual Apps at fau.edu/oit/instructional/virtual-apps/ or connect
   via VPN at fau.edu/oit/security/vpn/ to access campus resources remotely.

Q: How do I reset my FAUNet ID password?
A: Visit fau.edu/oit/services/accounts/ or call the Help Desk at 561-297-3999.

Q: I forgot my MyFAU login — what do I do?
A: Your MyFAU login uses your FAUNet ID. Contact the Help Desk at 561-297-3999
   for account recovery assistance.

Q: Is Microsoft Office free for FAU students?
A: Yes. FAU students have access to Microsoft 365 through their FAU account.

Q: Where are the computer labs on campus?
A: Visit fau.edu/oit/instructional/computer-labs/ for a full list of open
   computer lab locations and hours.

Q: How do I report a phishing or suspicious email?
A: Forward suspicious emails to FAU's security team and review guidance at
   fau.edu/oit/security/protect-yourself/

Q: What is the campus Wi-Fi network name?
A: FAU_WiFi is the primary wireless network on all FAU campuses.
