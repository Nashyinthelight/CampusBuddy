================================================================================
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: What are the gym hours during the semester?
A: The Recreation & Fitness Center is open Mon-Thu 6am-11pm, Fri 6am-10pm,
   Sat 8am-9pm, Sun 10am-9pm during fall and spring semesters.

Q: Is the pool open on weekends?
A: Saturday and Sunday the pool is closed in the morning but open 12:30-4pm.

Q: When can I use the climbing wall?
A: Open Climb is available Wednesday and Friday 4-6pm during fall and spring.

Q: What happens to gym hours during breaks?
A: Hours are reduced during winter, spring, and summer breaks. The RFC is open
   6am-8pm Mon-Fri, 10am-8pm Saturday, and 12pm-8pm Sunday during breaks.

Q: Is the gym open on holidays?
A: Most university holidays the RFC operates on reduced Holiday Hours
   (10am-6pm). Some holidays it is fully closed — check the adjusted hours
   schedule above or fau.edu/campusrec for the latest.

Q: How do I rent outdoor equipment?
A: Outdoor equipment rental through Base Camp is by appointment only. Email
   outdoor@fau.edu in advance to request a rental.

Q: Can I use the track during the day?
A: The track opens at 4:30pm on weekdays during fall and spring. During breaks
   it opens at 6am on weekdays.
