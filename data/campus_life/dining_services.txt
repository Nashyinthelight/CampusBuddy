MEAL PLANS
================================================================================

FAU offers meal plans for residential students, commuter/apartment students,
and employees on both Boca Raton and Jupiter campuses. All plans include access
to full-service dining halls with all-you-care-to-eat buffets.

For current pricing and plan details visit:
  Residential: dineoncampus.com/fau/residential-meal-plans
  Commuter/Apartment: dineoncampus.com/fau/commuter--apartment-meal-plans
  Meal plan FAQs: fau.edu/business-services/meal-plans/faq/

Note: Meal plan prices change each semester. Always check dineoncampus.com/fau
for current pricing before purchasing.

TYPES OF MEAL PLANS:
  Residential Meal Plans
    - For students living in on-campus residence halls
    - Include all-you-care-to-eat swipes at the Atlantic Dining Hall
    - Plans vary by number of swipes per week and Flex Bucks included
    - Meal plan swipes and Flex Bucks expire at the end of each semester

  Commuter / Apartment Meal Plans
    - For off-campus or apartment students
    - Block-based swipe plans available for use at the Atlantic Dining Hall
    - Larger blocks = lower cost per swipe

  Employee Meal Plans
    - Available to FAU faculty and staff

HOW MEAL PLAN CURRENCY WORKS:
  Meal Swipes: Used to access the Atlantic Dining Hall all-you-care-to-eat buffet
  Flex Bucks: Included with some meal plans; usable at dining hall and retail locations;
              expire at end of semester
  Owl Bucks: Separate declining balance loaded onto your Owl Card (see below)

OWL BUCKS:
  - Work like a debit card tied to your Owl Card
  - Accepted at any FAU dining location on campus including Starbucks, Chick-fil-A,
    Atlantic Dining Hall, Outtakes, Dunkin', Panda Express, and all Breezeway vendors
  - No minimum balance required
  - Balance carries forward until you graduate or leave FAU (does not expire each semester)
  - Can deposit funds at any time
  - Great flexible option for commuters and upperclassmen

GET APP (Meal Plan Mobile Management):
  Download on Google Play or Apple App Store, or register at get.cbord.com/fau

  Use the GET app to:
    - Check Owl Bucks and Flex Bucks balances
    - Review recent transactions
    - View Owl Card-accepted locations on campus
    - Report your Owl Card as lost or found

  To register for GET:
    1. You need an active meal plan
    2. Get your Owl Card's 7-digit ISO number by calling the Owl Card Center: 561-297-2700
    3. Download the app or go to get.cbord.com/fau
    4. Click "Sign up now!" and fill in your ISO number, last name, email, phone, and password

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Where can I check current dining hours?
A: Visit dineoncampus.com/fau/hours-of-operation for up-to-date hours.

Q: Where can I find current meal plan prices?
A: Prices change each semester. Check dineoncampus.com/fau/residential-meal-plans
   for residential plans or dineoncampus.com/fau/commuter--apartment-meal-plans
   for commuter plans.

Q: What is the difference between Flex Bucks and Owl Bucks?
A: Flex Bucks come bundled with certain meal plans and expire at the end of each
   semester. Owl Bucks are a separate declining balance on your Owl Card that never
   expire while you are enrolled and can be added at any time.

Q: Can I use my meal plan off campus?
A: No. Meal plans are valid at FAU dining locations on campus only.

Q: What if I don't have a meal plan?
A: You can pay with cash, credit card, or Owl Bucks at most dining locations.

Q: Do meal swipes and Flex Bucks roll over at the end of the semester?
A: No. Meal plan swipes and Flex Bucks expire at the end of each semester.
   Owl Bucks do carry forward.

Q: How do I check my Owl Bucks or Flex Bucks balance?
A: Use the GET app (get.cbord.com/fau). You need your Owl Card's 7-digit ISO
   number to register — call the Owl Card Center at 561-297-2700 to get it.

Q: Where can I find dining on the Jupiter campus?
A: Jupiter campus also has dining locations and convenience stores. Check
   dineoncampus.com/fau for Jupiter-specific options.

Q: How do I add Owl Bucks to my account?
A: Visit fau.edu/business-services/owl-card/owl-bucks/ for instructions.

Q: Who do I contact for dining questions?
A: Call Dining Services at 561-297-2019 or visit dineoncampus.com/FAU
