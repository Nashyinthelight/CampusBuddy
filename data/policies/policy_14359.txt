University Policy
2.2 Public Records Requests
Former Policy Number: 9.4
APPLICABILITY:
This policy applies to all Florida Atlantic University (“FAU” or “University”) campuses, institutes, divisions, colleges, departments, and employees.
DEFINITIONS:
Custodian of Public Records: Pursuant to University Regulation 1.005, the University President is the records custodian over all University records. The President may designate co-records custodians at his or her discretion. FAU’s Division of University Communications is designated as the Custodian of Public Records for purposes of § 119.12, Florida Statutes.
Public Records: All documents, papers, letters, maps, books, tapes, photographs, films, sound recordings, data processing software, or other material, regardless of physical form, characteristics, or means of transmission, made or received pursuant to law or ordinance or in connection with the transaction of official business by the University which are used to perpetuate, communicate or formalize knowledge.
POLICY STATEMENT:
The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for maintaining and responding to requests for public records, while also protecting those records that are exempt from Florida’s public records laws and/or protected from public disclosure by state or federal law. Public records in FAU’s custody shall be open for inspection by any person, at a reasonable time and under reasonable conditions, and FAU and its respective custodians of public records, shall furnish copies of public records upon payment of a charge, where authorized by law, for the cost of duplication and/or labor.
PROCEDURES:
FAU’s Division of University Communications is the designated office responsible for managing and overseeing requests made pursuant to Florida’s public records laws
(Chapter 119, Florida Statutes)
. A public records request may be submitted by email, online, fax, telephone or in person. A written request is not required; however, it is encouraged to prevent any misunderstanding about the nature or scope of the request. The contact information for FAU’s Custodian of Public Records is:
Florida Atlantic University

Boca Raton, FL 33431
Phone: 561-297-2452, Fax: 561-297-2307
Email:
publicrecords@fau.edu
Website:
https://www.fau.edu/public-affairs/media-relations/public-records/
If a University employee receives a public records request, they must promptly forward the request to FAU’s Public Records Specialist (
publicrecords@fau.edu
) for processing. The Public Records Specialist will send the requestor an acknowledgment email, assign a public record tracking number, and begin facilitating the process of retrieving the responsive records.
FAU shall respond to a public records request within a reasonable period of time after receiving the request. FAU is not required to reformat its records to provide them in a particular form as requested by the requestor. FAU may provide a copy of the requested record in the medium requested only if FAU maintains the record in that medium but may charge a fee to do so. FAU is not required to create new records to accommodate a request for information. FAU follows
Florida Governor’s Cost Recovery Polic
y and will assess charges for responding to a request for public records as authorized by law.
